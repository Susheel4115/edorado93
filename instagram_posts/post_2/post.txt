.
Fun Facts: 
👉 The character for “Biang” is considered to be one of the most complex Chinese characters. Depending on the variation, it has between 42 to 71 stokes. 
👉The name for the noodles, ‘biang biang’ is onomatopoeic, representing the sound the dough makes as it is stretched into long noodles by pulling the dough and slapping it against a surface.
